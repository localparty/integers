# Check CM4: CM curves are modular

**Group:** CM
**Source:** Paper 26 §11.1
**Pass criterion:** CM curves over ℚ have classical modularity
(pre-Wiles).

**Verdict:** PASS
**Rigor label:** [THEOREM]

**Justification:** Classical via Hecke theta series: for E with
CM by K, the Grössencharacter ψ determines a weight-2 Hecke
eigenform via f(z) = ∑ ψ(𝔞) e^{2πi N(𝔞) z}. This is due to
Deuring and Hecke, pre-dates Wiles 1995. No appeal to BCDT 2001
is needed for the CM case.
