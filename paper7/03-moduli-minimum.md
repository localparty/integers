# 3. The Flux Minimum and Gauge Coupling Unification

This section minimizes the G₄ flux potential `V_flux(r₂, r₃)` with
respect to both moduli and derives the radius ratio at the minimum as
a function of the flux quanta: `r₂/r₃ = F(n₁/n₂)`. The central
result is that the GUT unification condition `r₂/r₃ = √3/2` —
required for `α₃/α₂ = 1` at the compactification scale (Paper 4,
Appendix C) — is achieved for a specific rational flux ratio with
small numerator and denominator. Gauge coupling unification is thereby
derived from flux quantization: an integer condition on the G₄ field,
not a continuous fine-tuning of geometric parameters.
