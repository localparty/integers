# 2. The G₄ Flux Superpotential on CP² × S² × S¹/Z₂

This section constructs the explicit Gukov-Vafa-Witten superpotential
for M-theory compactified on the internal 7-manifold
`M₇ = CP² × S² × S¹/Z₂`. The two independent compact 4-cycles —
CP² (with flux quantum n₁) and `CP¹ × S²` (with flux quantum n₂) —
carry the integer-quantized G₄ flux that stabilizes the curved moduli.
We derive the Kahler potential for the CP² modulus r₃
(`K_{CP²} = −3 ln(r₃)`) and the S² modulus r₂
(`K_{S²} = −2 ln(r₂)`), construct the F-term potential in the 4D
N = 1 supergravity language, and verify that the flat S¹ direction
decouples from the flux sector — receiving no contribution from
W_GVW and remaining under the sole control of the Casimir mechanism
established in Paper 1.
