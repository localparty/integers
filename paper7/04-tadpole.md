# 4. The G₄ Tadpole Constraint

This section verifies that the flux configuration (n₁, n₂) required
for GUT unification satisfies the M-theory tadpole cancellation
condition `(1/(2π)²) ∫ G₄ ∧ G₄ + N_M2 = χ(X₈)/24`, where X₈ is the
8-manifold boundary in the Horava-Witten construction. We compute the
Euler characteristic `χ(X₈)/24` for the specific manifold, evaluate
the self-intersection numbers of the CP² and `CP¹ × S²` 4-cycles,
and confirm that the required flux quanta leave `N_M2 ≥ 0` — no
exotic anti-M2-brane charge is needed. The tadpole constraint also
bounds the total flux, placing an upper limit on n₁ and n₂ that is
consistent with the unification solution.
