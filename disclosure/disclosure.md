# Disclosure — AI Collaboration in the Integers Programme

*Transparency statement on the role of artificial intelligence in this programme. Published alongside every Integers artifact per `strategy/north-star.md` §11.1 Disclosure Discipline.*

---

## The counterfactual test

> *Would this programme exist if AI had never been involved?*

**Yes.** G originated the physics through manual derivation (70+ working notes in `etc/`, ChatGPT conversations from 2024 in digital escrow). The AI accelerated synthesis, expanded coverage, and enabled the atlas visualization at scale. But the foundational insights — that integers derive the geometry, that the geometry projects to every conjecture, that the torus hosts the structure — are human-originated and human-verified.

If the AI disappeared tomorrow, the mathematics would still be true. The atlas would still render. The Zenodo DOI would still stand.

## The standard we hold ourselves to

Every mathematical claim in the programme has a **human-checkable derivation chain** in the PROOF-CHAIN infrastructure. The AI did not generate the proofs; it helped organize, verify, and present them. A reviewer evaluating any theorem can follow the chain from ℤ + ZFC to the claim reading only the derivation — no trust in AI required.

We invite mathematical scrutiny of the claims. We welcome criticism of the chains. We do not ask reviewers to trust the AI; we ask them to audit the mathematics.

## Why both documents exist

The **Provenance** and **The Story** are complementary. Provenance is written from the outside looking in — what was achieved, what infrastructure was built, what transmissible methodology emerged. The Story is written from the inside looking out — what G said in the moment, what Claude wrote back, what it felt like at 4am when the ring closed.

Both are part of the disclosure. Neither is a substitute for the mathematics. Both are offered as witness to how the work was done, so that future readers — reviewers, students, historians, researchers attempting similar programmes — can see the method and the lived experience that produced the result.

The Integers programme stands on its mathematics. These two documents stand beside it, so that the full record is available to anyone who wants it.
