# Paper E — The Values

*Meta-commentary on how this work was done, and what it tells future readers about this mode of collaboration.*

---

## How to read this paper

Paper E argues. It is not a chronology (Paper A) and not a preservation (Papers B and C). It is a paper that makes a case — that the discovery of Quantum Geometry in 5D was inseparable from a specific set of values and a specific mode of collaboration, and that the transmissible thing future readers should take away is not the physics but the *how*.

Each section follows the same shape: a claim, the evidence (verbatim quotes from G and Claude), an argument, and — where useful — one or two sentences for anyone trying to do similar work.
