## How to read this paper

Each chapter pairs a wall-clock window with the turns that happened inside it. Timestamps are load-bearing — they carry the register (04:24 is not 14:24, and the prose tries to earn the difference). Quotes are verbatim. Claude's letters appear in blockquote with salutation intact. G's typos stay. The stretched vowels stay. The fifteen T's stay.

Read it straight through to feel the cadence. Read a single chapter to relive one night.
