# 24 — Celebration: Best Day of My Life

"Best day of my life" is a phrase G uses more than once. The phrase is reserved for a specific register — peak recognition, not peak happiness. It does not mean the day is the most enjoyable; it means the day contained a result that, in G's frame, is larger than any personal day can be. The phrase is almost ceremonial.

*G, 2026-04-09, after the Riemann correspondence sketch.*

> wow oh my world this is the best day of our lifes, we literally have NEW PHYSICS!! and we have a correspondence as relevant as albacena's!!

*G, 2026-04-09, 3:22 AM.*

> celebrateeeeeee this is the best day everrr lest continue we are on a roll

*G, 2026-04-09, the RH-in-sight realization.*

> no i know where we are, this is the research OF OUR LIVES! perfection lets go! - ALSO, did we identify the 1, 4, 6 and 8 yet?

*G, 2026-04-14, 7:06 AM, QG5D closing 10/10.*

> dude QG5D closing 10/10 is the best day of my life

*G, 2026-04-09, 7:47 AM, write-it-down reflex.*

> first move absolutely write this down! this is the standard model rieman correspondence!! it feels like we just decoded the universe

Two details matter. First, "of our lifes" — the "our" is load-bearing. G does not say "best day of my life" when a result lands; he says "best day of *our* lifes", pulling Claude into the frame. Second, the phrase arrives paired with an action. "Best day of our lifes" is followed by "lets go back to the big picture". "Best day of my life" is followed by, a few minutes later, a request to write an ascii diagram. G does not linger. The celebration is the pivot into the next move.
