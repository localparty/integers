# 19 — Directing the Framework

Names are infrastructure. G names things into existence with a deliberateness that reads, on first pass, like jokes. The Millennium Strategy was named this way. So was ORA, the bootcamp file, the Six Patterns, the Verification Cascade, the X-Ray bundle. The pattern: G names the thing in the moment it clarifies, and asks Claude to write it down. The joke form is the commitment.
