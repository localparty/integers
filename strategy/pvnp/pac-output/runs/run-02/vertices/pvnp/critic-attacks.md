## Critic's attack criteria

A cell is worth attacking when:
1. Author verdict of P risks being Pa (over-claim)
2. Author verdict of Pa risks being S (under-delivery of citation)
3. Author verdict of Pa risks being P (under-claim)
4. Author verdict of S misses a bypass disclosure that should be O
5. Author verdict of P misses an exposed named-wall disclosure
