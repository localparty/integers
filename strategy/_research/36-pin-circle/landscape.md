## Major Programmes (with landscape context)

### 1. Higgs Precision at HL-LHC (2029-2041)
Target: 3 ab^{-1}; 3%-7% precision on rare Higgs decays. Tests Higgs sector completeness — directly relevant to 5D-geometry prediction of Higgs as 5D component.

### 2. Dark Energy Time Evolution (DESI/Euclid/Roman/Rubin)
DESI DR2 (2025): w_0, w_a deviation from -1 at ~4.2σ. If confirmed at 5σ by Euclid+Roman+DESI combo (~2028), ΛCDM broken. QG5D prediction: geometric origin of dark energy.

### 3. Neutrino Mass / CP Violation (DUNE, Hyper-K)
Absolute mass scale, mass hierarchy, δ_CP. Relevant to seesaw mechanism predictions in 5D.

### 4. Gravitational Wave Background
O5 (~2027+) will reach 3× sensitivity; LISA 2035. PTA stochastic already detected at NANOGrav. Cosmological strings, first-order phase transitions.

### 5. JWST Early Universe Puzzles
High-z bright galaxies, "little red dots." May require dynamical dark energy or modified structure formation.

### 6. Muon g−2 Closure
Discrepancy between lattice (BMW) and dispersive (BNL/Fermilab) methods is ~2.5σ. Resolution expected by 2027 with new data.

We honor every collaboration producing data the ring compares against. We take no credit for their measurements; our contribution is the theoretical prediction and its position within the ring.
