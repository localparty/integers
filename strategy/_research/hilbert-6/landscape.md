## Major Approaches

### 1. Boltzmann → Fluid Derivation (Deng–Hani–Ma 2025)
Derive Euler / NS from particle systems via Boltzmann kinetic theory — resolves the fluid-dynamics aspect of H6. Builds on Lanford (1975), Gallagher–Saint-Raymond–Texier, Bodineau–Gallagher–Saint-Raymond. Critiqued by follow-up (Simonella et al. 2025).

### 2. Algebraic / Constructive QFT (Wightman, Haag–Kastler, Osterwalder–Schrader)
Axiomatize QFT. Wightman axioms, Haag-Kastler algebraic approach, OS axioms (Euclidean). Partial progress on specific models.

### 3. Category-Theoretic / Higher Structures (Schreiber, Freed-Hopkins)
Formalize physics via higher category theory, cohomology, modular categories. Classifies gapped phases (Kong–Wen), TQFTs (Atiyah–Segal–Lurie).

### 4. Locally Covariant QFT (Brunetti-Fredenhagen-Verch)
Category-theoretic axioms for QFT on curved spacetimes.

### 5. Probability Foundations (Kolmogorov 1933, completed part of H6)
Axiomatic probability (part of original H6); considered solved.

### 6. GR Foundations
Einstein's axiomatic formulation; modern mathematical GR (Choquet-Bruhat).

### 7. Holographic / AdS-CFT Axioms
Post-2000 programme: holographic principle as physics axiom. Maldacena (1997). Emerging axiom framework.
