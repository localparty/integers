## Major Approaches

### 1. Inter-Universal Teichmüller Theory (Mochizuki; Fesenko)
Mochizuki's 2012 series of 4 papers claiming ABC. Scholze–Stix 2018 report: gap in Corollary 3.12. Mochizuki disagrees. Community verdict remains: ABC conjecture is unresolved via IUT.

### 2. Joshi's Framework (2021+)
Kirti Joshi has developed an alternative formalization of the IUT ideas. Ongoing dialogue with Scholze.

### 3. Vojta's Conjecture / Diophantine Geometry
ABC as special case of Vojta; linking to Mordell (Faltings 1983), Lang-Vojta, Bombieri.

### 4. Effective ABC (weak versions)
Known: rad(abc)^{κ} ≥ max(|a|,|b|,|c|) for κ = 3 (Stewart-Tijdeman) and refinements. Inefficient but unconditional.

### 5. Computational
Van Frankenhuysen searches for ABC triples with high quality. Reken mee met ABC distributed project.

### Our programme's position
The ring's ABC vertex has very low confidence (1/10). Our approach is *not* via IUT; we use the Bost–Connes spectral framework applied to rad(abc) statistics, bounded by moment estimates from the adèle-class-space. Complementary to all existing approaches; conditional on CCM. We explicitly do not take a position on the Mochizuki–Scholze–Stix–Joshi debate; we cite all parties and acknowledge the controversy.
