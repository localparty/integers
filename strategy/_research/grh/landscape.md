## Major Approaches

### 1. Computational Verification (Platt)
Platt verified GRH for primitive characters to height ~3.8×10^13; >29 billion L-functions checked. No counterexamples. Necessary-not-sufficient.

### 2. NCG / CCM Extension
Connes–Consani–Marcolli programme naturally accommodates Dirichlet characters; arithmetic site has χ-twists. The bridge construction turns each χ into a module-level symmetry.

### 3. Weak GRH / Zero-Density / Average (Soundararajan, Harper, Radziwiłł)
Establish GRH on average; Bombieri–Vinogradov-type results. Log-free zero-density.

### 4. Patterson-Style Conjectural Arithmetic Consequences (Dunn–Radziwiłł 2021)
Patterson's cubic Gauss conjecture proved conditionally on GRH. Shows GRH's deep arithmetic consequences.

### 5. Function Field Analogs (Weil's Theorem for Curves over F_q)
Weil 1948: GRH for L-functions of curves over finite fields (the "geometric GRH"). Foundation for Connes-Consani "characteristic 1" approach.

### Our programme's position
The ring's GRH vertex treats each Dirichlet character χ as a symmetry on the CCM adèle-class-space; the bridge-χ construction shows that the spectral realization of L(s, χ) inherits RH structure from ζ if and only if a specific χ-compatibility hypothesis holds. Level 2 (conditional on CCM) is proved in our current chain. Every author listed here contributes either a compatibility check, a parallel piece of evidence, or an external dependency.
