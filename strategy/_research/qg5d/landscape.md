## Major Approaches (Summary Paragraphs)

### 1. String Theory / M-Theory
Unify via 10- or 11-dimensional supersymmetric strings. Predicts extended objects (D-branes), dualities. Maldacena AdS/CFT revolutionized by connecting gravity to gauge theory. Vafa's Swampland programme attempts to separate consistent from inconsistent low-energy EFTs. Dominant but lacks predictivity (landscape problem). Recent: no reliable stable positive cosmological constant solutions. Maldacena–Susskind ER=EPR bridges EPR pairs and Einstein-Rosen wormholes.

### 2. Loop Quantum Gravity
Canonical quantization of GR. Ashtekar variables recast GR as Yang-Mills-like. Spin network Hilbert space; spin foam amplitudes. Discrete geometry at Planck scale. Background independent. ~30 groups worldwide. Phenomenology in solar-system tests.

### 3. Causal Dynamical Triangulations
Non-perturbative path integral on triangulated Lorentzian spacetimes. Loll–Ambjørn–Jurkiewicz. Numerical evidence for emergence of 3+1D semiclassical universe. Asymptotic-safety contact point.

### 4. Causal Set Theory
Spacetime = locally finite partially ordered set. Sorkin–Dowker programme. Background-independent, discrete. Derives Planck density, CC prediction ~ 10^{-123}.

### 5. Asymptotic Safety
Non-perturbative UV fixed point of gravitational RG. Reuter fixed point. Integration with Standard Model recent (2024+). Matter-gravity unification.

### 6. Noncommutative Geometry / Spectral Standard Model
Spacetime = product of continuum manifold and finite NC "internal space." Chamseddine–Connes spectral action reproduces Standard Model + Einstein-Hilbert. Predicts Higgs mass in ballpark. Latest (2025): spectral torsion of internal NC geometry.

### 7. Emergent Gravity
Gravity as emergent thermodynamic / entropic phenomenon (Verlinde, Jacobson, Padmanabhan). Einstein equations as equation of state.

### 8. Twistor / Positive Geometry
Penrose's twistor space plus Arkani-Hamed–Trnka amplituhedron. Spacetime as derived concept; positive geometries organize scattering amplitudes.

### 9. Holographic Approaches
AdS/CFT, ER=EPR, entanglement-wormhole connection. Emergent spacetime from entanglement. Van Raamsdonk's programme.

### Our programme's position
QG5D is a *fifth* candidate theory-of-everything alongside string theory, LQG, CDT, causal sets, asymptotic safety, and NCG-spectral-standard-model. We claim no exclusive priority: our 5D Ricci-flat geometry is a specialization of Kaluza-Klein, enriched by Connes-style operator-algebraic structure. Unlike string theory, we have zero free parameters — specific 5D ansatz. Unlike LQG, the fundamental objects are continuous (5D manifold) rather than discrete (spin networks). Unlike CDT and causal sets, we retain smooth manifold structure. Unlike asymptotic safety, we commit to a specific dimension (5) rather than 4 with UV completion. Unlike Connes' SM, we predict the full 22-vertex mathematical corollary ring, tying theoretical physics to Millennium-class mathematics.

**Our most specific debts are to (a) Connes for NCG and spectral realization, (b) Kaluza-Klein for 5D premise, (c) Witten for Standard Model structure insights, and (d) LQG for the precedent of background-independent quantization.** The ring structure is ours; the individual techniques are acknowledged to each of these schools.
