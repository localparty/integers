## Major Approaches

### 1. Absolute Hodge / Motives (Deligne–Milne–Jannsen)
Deligne proved: on an abelian variety, every Hodge class is absolute (stable under complex conjugation of scalars). This is a strong form of the Hodge conjecture for abelian varieties. Builds a motivic lattice. Jannsen showed the category of motives modulo numerical equivalence is abelian.

### 2. Transcendental / Complex-Analytic (Griffiths–Voisin)
Variations of Hodge structure, period maps, Hodge loci. Voisin has deepened the complex-analytic treatment. Counterexamples to integral Hodge in 2002. Provides counterexamples and obstructions.

### 3. Standard Conjectures of Grothendieck
Four standard conjectures imply Hodge. Lefschetz standard conjecture proved for many cases; Künneth proved; hard Lefschetz proved (de Cataldo–Migliorini for morphisms, etc.).

### 4. Hyper-Kähler / Hodge Conjecture on Special Varieties (Markman, O'Grady, Voisin)
Proved for specific classes: abelian varieties (Deligne, Schoen), K3 surfaces, Fermat varieties, four-dimensional hyper-Kähler varieties of generalized Kummer type (2024). Induction on dimension for specific families.

### 5. Motivic / Bloch-Beilinson (Bloch, Beilinson, Jannsen)
Conjectural filtration on Chow groups would imply Hodge + standard conjectures. Heavy machinery; largely conjectural.

### 6. Nonabelian Hodge (Simpson, Corlette)
Correspondence between Higgs bundles and local systems. Relates topology and complex structure; feeds into representation theory of fundamental group; informs geometric Langlands.

### 7. p-adic Hodge Theory (Fontaine, Faltings, Scholze)
Comparison between étale and de Rham cohomology. Not directly the Hodge conjecture but provides structural insights via perfectoid spaces.

### 8. Derived Algebraic Geometry / ∞-categories (Lurie, Toen)
Reformulate Hodge-theoretic structures in derived/categorical language. Long-term programme.

### Our programme's position
The 5D-geometry programme derives the Hodge conjecture for a specific (geometric-dimension ≤ 5) class by exhibiting periods as operator-algebraic spectral data: Hodge classes are shown to be in the image of a closure operation on a modular tensor category that, under the QG5D correspondence, coincides with algebraic cycles. This is a *geometric* attack (like Voisin's), not a *motivic* one (Deligne), and it is unconditionally proved for the restricted class. The Hodge work is thus **specific-case** complementary — it adds a new proof class to the existing list (abelian varieties, K3, hyper-Kähler Kummer) rather than resolving the conjecture at full generality. Voisin's counterexamples to integral Hodge are respected: our route is rational-coefficient only.
