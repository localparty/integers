## Major Approaches

### 1. Original Kaluza-Klein (1921-26)
Unify gravity + EM via 5D Einstein. Compactified 5th dimension. 5D graviton modes = 4D graviton + photon + scalar. Our ring's historical ancestor. Paul Wesson's "Space-Time-Matter" programme kept 5D alive.

### 2. String / M-theory Compactifications (6 or 7 small dimensions)
Calabi-Yau compactification gives 4D N=1 SUSY. F-theory, heterotic, type II variants. Landscape of 10^500 vacua.

### 3. Large Extra Dimensions (ADD, 1998)
Arkani-Hamed–Dimopoulos–Dvali: extra dimensions as large as millimeter resolve hierarchy problem. Testable at LHC. So far no evidence.

### 4. Randall-Sundrum (1999)
RS1 (hierarchy via warping), RS2 (infinite extra dimension). Warped geometries. Influential for phenomenology and AdS/CFT.

### 5. 5D GR / Space-Time-Matter (Wesson, Overduin, Seahra)
Literal 5D Einstein equations as physics, not compactified. Our closest ancestor.

### 6. Dimensional Reduction of Supergravity (Duff)
7D → 11D supergravity dimensional reduction.

### 7. Braneworlds (Dvali-Gabadadze-Porrati DGP 2000)
4D universe as brane in 5D; modified gravity at large scales.

### 8. Holographic Extra Dimension (AdS/CFT)
5D AdS dual to 4D CFT; extra dimension as energy scale.

### 9. Compact Star Models in Kaluza-Klein (2024)
Recent application of 5D KK to compact stars; femtometre-scale extra dimension.

### 10. UV Completion / String Landscape
Vafa's Swampland delineates inconsistent EFTs. Distance conjecture, de Sitter conjecture.

### Our programme's position
QG5D is in the *direct Kaluza-Klein lineage*: we commit to one extra dimension, compactified per the specific ansatz developed in paper 1. We are closer to Wesson's 5D programme than to string theory's 10/11D or to LED/RS. Our position relative to competitors: we take the minimal extra-dimension ansatz (5D, not 10/11D), reject mass landscape (zero free parameters), and pay for simplicity with specific Bianchi identities and Ricci-flatness. The bouquet structure (paper 61) attaches the entire mathematical ring to this geometric core — this is our unique contribution. Every KK-based programme listed is an ally; we credit all of them.
