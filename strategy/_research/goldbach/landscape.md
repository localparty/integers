## Major Approaches

### 1. Circle Method (Hardy–Littlewood → Vinogradov → Helfgott)
Hardy–Littlewood (1923): conditional asymptotic for strong Goldbach. Vinogradov (1937): every large odd is sum of three primes, unconditionally. Helfgott (2013): unconditional for odd ≥ 7 — completes ternary.

### 2. Sieve Methods (Brun → Chen)
Chen (1966): every large even integer is p + (semi-prime). Best known approximation to strong Goldbach.

### 3. Computational Verification
Oliveira e Silva, Platt: strong Goldbach verified up to 4 × 10^18. No counterexamples.

### 4. Weak/Refined Results
Ramaré: every even ≥ 4 is sum of at most 6 primes (1995). Tao (2014): every odd is sum of at most 5 primes.

### 5. Short Intervals / Arithmetic Progressions
Goldbach in short intervals, in AP modulo q.
