## Major Approaches

### 1. Density Results (Krasikov, Lagarias, Terras, Tao)
Krasikov–Lagarias: density of n ≤ x reaching 1 is ≥ x^0.84. Tao (2019): *almost all* orbits attain almost-bounded values (logarithmic density).

### 2. Dynamical Systems / 2-adic
Treat Collatz as dynamical system on 2-adic integers; ergodicity questions.

### 3. Computational Verification
Verified to very large heights (≥ 2^68); no counterexamples.

### 4. Cycle Analysis
Best-known lower bound on any non-trivial cycle length: 355504839929.

### 5. Generalizations / Turing Problem
Antihydra problem (2024): six-state Turing machine whose halting reduces to Collatz-like question. Strong hint of undecidability for generalizations.

### Our programme's position
Our ring targets Collatz via harmonic analysis on the 2-adic integers, using a spectral realization that places the Collatz map in an operator-algebraic setting. Complementary to Tao's probabilistic picture. Low confidence (far from operationally verified).
