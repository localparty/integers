## 2. 2D Yang–Mills on CP¹ and its partition function

### 2.1 The Migdal heat-kernel form

For a 2D Yang–Mills theory on a closed oriented surface Σ of genus g
with gauge group G and area A, the exact Migdal partition function is

$$
  Z_{G}(A, g; \Sigma)
    \;=\; \sum_{R\in\widehat G}\,(\dim R)^{\,2-2g}\,
           \exp\!\bigl(-\tfrac{1}{2}\,e^{2}\,A\,C_{2}(R)\bigr),
$$

where R runs over irreducible representations of G, dim R is the
representation dimension, and C_2(R) is the quadratic Casimir.

For Σ = CP¹ = S² (g = 0) and G = SU(3), the sum collapses to

$$
  Z_{SU(3)}(A, 0; S^{2})
    \;=\; \sum_{R\in\widehat{SU(3)}}\,(\dim R)^{\,2}\,
           \exp\!\bigl(-\tfrac{1}{2}\,e^{2}\,A\,C_{2}(R)\bigr).
$$

The dominant term in the A → 0 limit is R = adjoint (dim = 8, C_2 = 3
in SU(3)), giving a contribution 8² · exp(−(3/2) e² A).

### 2.2 Mellin transform

Take the Mellin transform in the dimensionless area variable
α := (1/2) e² A:

$$
  \widetilde Z_{SU(3)}(s)
    \;:=\; \int_{0}^{\infty}\,\alpha^{\,s-1}\,Z_{SU(3)}(\alpha)\,d\alpha
    \;=\; \Gamma(s)\,\sum_{R}\,\frac{(\dim R)^{2}}{C_2(R)^{s}}.
$$

This is a Dirichlet series indexed by SU(3) irreps, weighted by
(dim R)² / C_2(R)^s. The structure is strikingly parallel to the
Bost–Connes partition function

$$
  Z_{\mathrm{BC}}(\beta) \;=\; \mathrm{Tr}(e^{-\beta H_{\mathrm{BC}}})
                        \;=\; \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\,\frac{1}{n^{\beta}}
                        \;=\; \zeta(\beta),
$$

where n ∈ N* labels the BC basis states |μ_n Ω⟩ and H_BC = log N̂.

## 6. The perturbative system as an asymptotic state

### 6.2 The perturbative state ω_pert

**Definition 6.1.** The **perturbative state** ω_pert is the β → 0⁺

$$
  \omega_{\mathrm{pert}} \;:=\; \lim_{\beta \to 0^{+}}\,\omega_{\beta},

with equal weight, interpreted as the Dixmier-trace-regularised

**Proof sketch.** The state ω_pert averages over all n with uniform
