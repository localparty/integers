  Establishes the e-circle $S^1$, quantum phase as e-coordinate,
  perturbative finiteness via zeta regularization.

  Tensions, and Thirteen Observables from Kaluza--Klein Geometry."
  Cosmological predictions from the framework.

- Paper 3: "Information Preservation in Black Hole Evaporation via
  e-Dimension Geometry." Resolution of the information paradox.

- Paper 4: "From the e-Circle to the Standard Model: Gauge Group
  Selection by Entanglement Geometry." Extension to
  $M^4 \times \mathbb{CP}^2 \times S^2 \times S^1$ and the full
  Standard Model gauge group.

- Paper 5: "Color Confinement, the Strong Force, and the CP$^2$
  Holonomy." Confinement from $\mathbb{CP}^2$ topology, string tension,
  proton mass.

- Paper 6: "The Complete Thermal History from Inflation to Dark Energy."
  Dilaton-driven cosmological evolution.
