## 33.2. Why H4 is uniquely HIGH liability

Four features make H4 uniquely HIGH liability among the remaining conditionals:

1. **High-confidence face.** CURVATURE is at 9.5/10 — the highest of all ten faces. The H4 wall is therefore the most *visible* remaining gap: it's the one that, when closed, delivers a 10/10 face and a full Clay Millennium closure.

2. **Well-specified external statement.** H4 is not a programme-internal axiom (like the CBB axioms) or an infrastructural gap (like Goldbach's circle-method deficit). It is a specific mathematical statement about the match between perturbative and non-perturbative QFT — the kind of statement that, once proved, closes cleanly.

3. **Identified attack strategy.** The S-duality diagnostic identifies three concrete routes (§30, §31). Unlike Goldbach's 1/10 (no identified route), H4 has a well-defined parallel-attack structure.

4. **Clay Millennium payoff.** Closing H4 delivers Clay Millennium #4 (YM mass gap). Closing most other conditionals delivers programme-internal strengthenings without Clay-class payoff.

Paper 49 + Paper 50 together close two of the four Clay-relevant programme conditionals (CCM for RH, H4 for YM). This is the structural pairing: two Clay Prizes in two papers, each removing a HIGH-liability face. The X-ray diagnostic says: after Papers 49 + 50, no conditional of comparable Clay-relevance and X-ray visibility remains.

## 33.6. The verdict's scope limit

The X-ray verdict says H4 is the top-priority conditional to close, and Paper 50 executes this priority. What the verdict does *not* say:

- It does not say H4 will close in 12 months. The confidence is 3-4/10 initially, and the realistic horizon is 12-30 months.
- It does not say Paper 50 closes H4 with certainty. The three-route strategy is insurance, not a guarantee.
- It does not say other conditionals are unimportant. Goldbach, Selberg, Lehmer, Cramér remain programme priorities after Paper 50.
- It does not say the programme is done after Paper 50. The CBB axioms themselves remain programme-internal conditionals that the Robustness-Circle Theorem (§41) must address.

The verdict is about *priority*, not about *sufficiency*. Paper 50 is the right paper to write now. After Paper 50, other papers follow with their own priorities. The programme's work extends beyond the two-paper unit (49 + 50) even if the two-paper unit is the most consequential.
