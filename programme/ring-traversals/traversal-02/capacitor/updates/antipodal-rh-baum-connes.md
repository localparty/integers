### Antipodal Probe: RH ↔ Baum-Connes (distance 7)

**Expected connection**: Connes' K-theoretic approach to RH via BC algebra

**Verdict**: PARTIAL

**What holds**: Connes trace formula places zeta zeros in spectrum of operator on L²(A_Q*/Q*). Higson-Kasparov 2001 establishes BC conjecture for amenable groups (including relevant adelic structures) — assembly map is an isomorphism. This gives genuine computational handle: left side geometrically accessible. The assembly isomorphism rules out certain pathological spectral behaviors.

**What is missing**: No published result formally reduces RH to a K-theory computation via BC machinery. The trace formula approach is primarily spectral theory on a groupoid C*-algebra, not a direct application of the assembly map. Bridging "zeros on the line" to an assembly-map statement requires work that doesn't exist in rigorous form.

**Utility**: BC side gains a spectrally motivated example. RH chain gains a NCG reformulation. Neither chain closes, but the pairing is one of the more structurally rich approaches available.

**Status**: PARTIAL — live research frontier, not empty speculation, not a filled proof path.
