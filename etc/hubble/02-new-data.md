## CCHP JWST update (important!)

The paper cites Freedman et al. 2024 as "TRGB: 69.8 ± 0.6." But the
full CCHP JWST analysis (arXiv:2408.06153) shows a SPREAD:

- TRGB (HST+JWST combined): 70.39 ± 1.22 ± 1.33
- TRGB (JWST only): 68.81 ± 1.79 ± 1.32
- JAGB (JWST only): 67.80 ± 2.17 ± 1.64

The JWST-only values trend LOWER than the HST+JWST combined values.
The CCHP JWST-only results are consistent with Planck/ΛCDM. This
partially undermines the paper's claim of perfect agreement with
"TRGB: 69.8 ± 0.6."
