# AE1 — Overlaps are analytic functions of λ

**Claim:** The overlap f_N(λ) = ⟨φ_0(λ) | a(λ)⟩ is real-analytic.

**Pass criterion:** analyticity established; domain clear.

**Finding:**
- Matrix entries of A^{ev}(λ, N) are real-analytic in λ (rational
  + digamma + hypergeometric, all analytic where defined).
- By Kato-Rellich analytic perturbation theory, simple eigenvectors
  are real-analytic in a parameter wherever the spectral gap is
  positive.
- The archimedean vector a(λ) is rational in L = 2 log λ, hence
  real-analytic in λ for λ > 1.
- Therefore f_N is real-analytic on (1, ∞) where the gap is
  positive (almost everywhere).

**Status:** PASS.

**Confidence:** 9/10.
