# BG5 — No spectral pollution

**Claim:** Bögli's theorem excludes spurious eigenvalues.

**Pass criterion:** no eigenvalue drift; no spectrum creeps in; no continuous spectrum appears.

**Finding:**
- Bögli's theorem (under H1 + H2) excludes spurious eigenvalues
  and losses: (i) every limit of spec(D_N) is in spec(D_∞),
  (ii) every point of spec(D_∞) is a limit of spec(D_N).
- Continuous spectrum: Bögli's theorem is silent about whether
  D_∞ has continuous spectrum. Paper 13 argues D_∞ has compact
  resolvent (Corollary 9.8), hence discrete spectrum, hence no
  continuous spectrum. This relies on the uniform H¹ bound
  surviving the limit.
- Conditional on ES3 (Theorem 7.1) holding at the relevant λ,
  compact resolvent holds, continuous spectrum is ruled out.

**Status:** PASS (at fixed λ = √14, conditional on ES3).

**Confidence:** 7/10.
