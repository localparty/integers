# HZ4 — Zeros of Ξ = {γ_n}

**Claim:** Ξ(t) := ξ(1/2 + it) has zeros exactly at γ_n, imaginary
parts of non-trivial zeros of ζ.

**Pass criterion:** all non-trivial zeros captured.

**Finding:**
- Standard fact about the Riemann Xi function. Ξ is entire,
  with zeros {ρ − 1/2}/i for all non-trivial zeros ρ of ζ.
- For a zero ρ = 1/2 + iγ on the critical line, the
  corresponding zero of Ξ is at t = γ (real).
- For a hypothetical zero ρ = 1/2 + δ + iγ off the critical
  line, the corresponding zero of Ξ is at t = γ − iδ (complex,
  |Im t| = |δ| < 1/2 in the critical strip).
- All such zeros lie in {|Im z| < 1/2}, and Hurwitz (if
  applied correctly) captures every zero in that domain.

**Status:** PASS.

**Confidence:** 10/10 (standard).
