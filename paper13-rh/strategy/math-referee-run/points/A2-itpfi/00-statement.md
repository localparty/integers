# A2 — ITPFI Factorization [LIGHT]

## Claim (Paper 13 Theorem 4.1)

> The unique KMS₁ state ω₁ on the Bost–Connes C*-algebra admits
> a factorization ω₁ = ⊗_p ω₁^{(p)} as an infinite tensor product
> of p-local KMS₁ states.

Three independent proofs are given:

1. **Proof 1 (KMS uniqueness chain):** Laca–Raeburn 1996
   (p-local KMS₁ uniqueness) + Bratteli–Robinson Proposition
   5.3.23 (product of KMS states is KMS) + Bost–Connes 1995
   Theorem 25 (global KMS₁ uniqueness at β = 1).

2. **Proof 2 (amenability + Bauer simplex):** Laca–Neshveyev 2004
   for amenable semigroup crossed products.

3. **Proof 3 (Araki–Woods ITPFI classification):** each p-local
   GNS factor is type III_{1/p}, and the infinite tensor product
   of type III_{λ_p} factors is an ITPFI factor of type III₁.

Corollary 4.2: ω₁^{(≤P_N)} → ω₁ weak-*.

Proposition 4.3: Entry-by-entry convergence of the Weil
quadratic form Q_N(f, g) → Q_∞(f, g) for all f, g in the
algebraic direct limit.
