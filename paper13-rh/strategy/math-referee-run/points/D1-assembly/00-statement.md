# D1 — The Assembly [HEAVY]

## The final argument

Paper 13 Theorem 10.3 / Section 11.2 chains six layers:

1. **Layer 1:** CCM operators D_N on E_N^+, self-adjoint, with
   zeros of ξ̂_N = spec(D_N) (CCM Theorem 5.10(iii)).

2. **Layer 2:** ITPFI factorization gives weak-* convergence and
   entry-by-entry form stabilization.

3. **Layer 3:** Four estimates (archimedean, eigenvector
   approximation via ITPFI triangle, uniform H¹, CF decay).

4. **Layer 4:** Teschl form bound + Bögli spectral exactness
   → spec(D_∞) = lim spec(D_N), no spurious eigenvalues.

5. **Layer 5:** Hurwitz + ξ̂_N → Ξ uniformly on compacts
   → lim spec(D_N) = {γ_n}.

6. **Layer 6:** spec(D_∞) = {γ_n} ⊂ ℝ (by self-adjointness)
   → γ_n ∈ ℝ for all n → RH.

This point interrogates the chain as a whole, not its individual
links (which are interrogated in A1–C2).
