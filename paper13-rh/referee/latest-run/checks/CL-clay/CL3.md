# CL3: CBB axioms proved or reducible to standard results
**FAIL.** Axiom 1 (spectral realisation) goes beyond published results. Meyer 2005 gives distributional spectral inclusion; Axiom 1 asserts genuine point spectrum with compact resolvent. This is a strictly stronger claim not established in the literature.

Axiom 4 (bridge family) is proved at k=3 and apparently at k=4,6 (proofs written out in Section 3.4-3.5). This is closer to meeting the criterion.
