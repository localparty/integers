# D2.02: CBB Conditionality

The proof skeleton's "Honest accounting" states: "The proof depends on the CBB axioms." Section 11.6 identifies two residuals. The revised version (v2) claims to resolve Residual B via Meyer-Nelson compatibility.

**Assessment:** The claim that the proof is unconditional (v2) is not supported. The Meyer-Nelson compatibility argument (research/266, Section 1.3, Appendix B.5) asserts but does not prove the upgrade from distributional to genuine spectrum. Axiom 1 remains an assumption.

The honest accounting in the proof skeleton is MORE accurate than the revised Section 14, which claims unconditional status. The original conditional framing was correct.

**Verdict: The proof is CONDITIONAL on the CBB axioms, specifically Axiom 1 (spectral realisation) and Axiom 4 (bridge family).**
