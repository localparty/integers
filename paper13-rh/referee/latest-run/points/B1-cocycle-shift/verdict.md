# B1 Verdict: The Cocycle Shift Formula

**The algebraic formula:** CORRECT. The expression (1 - p^{-2*delta})/(p - p^{-2*delta}) is an exact closed form with the stated properties.

**The derivation:** The algebra is correct. The interpretation as a "cocycle shift" has conceptual gaps.

**The integrality constraint:** NOT RIGOROUSLY JUSTIFIED. The paper does not prove that the change in a KMS evaluation corresponds to a discrete shift in a cohomology class.

**The logical chain:** Two gaps: (a) the bridge lemma persistence under perturbation, and (b) the integrality constraint.

**Overall: FAIL.** The mechanism is creative but the connection between continuous KMS evaluation changes and discrete cohomological invariants is not proved.
