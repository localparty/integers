# A2: Meyer Spectral Inclusion [HEAVY]

**Location:** Section 1.3, Section 9.1, Appendix B

**Claim:** Meyer (2005) proved the Riemann zeros lie in the distributional spectrum of T_BC. The proof upgrades this to genuine eigenvalues of the self-adjoint closure T-bar_BC via "approximate spectrum preservation." This is THE most critical technical claim in the entire paper.
