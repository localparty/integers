This paper develops the **Gukov-Vafa-Witten (GVW) G₄ flux
superpotential** for M-theory compactified on CP² × S² × S¹/Z₂.
The four-form G₄ couples to the curved 4-cycles (CP² and CP¹ × S²)

    n₂/n₁ = −17/9     (smallest integers: n₁ = 9, n₂ = −17)
