# arXiv Withdrawal and Versions Policy

**Source:** https://info.arxiv.org/help/withdraw.html
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## When papers can be withdrawn
- **Before announcement:** May be deleted or delayed via user page.
- **After announcement:** Cannot be completely removed.

## What happens on withdrawal
- A new version marked "withdrawn" is created.
- Withdrawn version becomes default view without direct PDF/TeX download.
- Previous versions remain accessible, including the full text.

## Who can withdraw
- Authors, via their user page (withdraw icon per paper).

## Inappropriate reasons for withdrawal
> It is not appropriate to withdraw a paper because it is published or submitted to a journal. [Use journal-reference submission feature instead.]
>
> It is not appropriate to withdraw a paper because it is being updated. [Use replacement feature instead.]

## Implication for QG5D
- arXiv submissions are effectively PERMANENT. Plan accordingly.
- Do NOT submit scaffolding/preliminary drafts to arXiv — use Zenodo for early-stage deposits.
- Only submit papers that are Clay-caliber or journal-caliber.
