# arXiv Submission Rules

**Source:** https://info.arxiv.org/help/submit/index.html
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## Accepted formats (in order of preference)
1. **(La)TeX, AMS(La)TeX, PDFLaTeX** — recommended for portability
2. **PDF**
3. **HTML with JPEG/PNG/GIF images**

Explicitly rejected:
- DVI, PS, or PDF created from TeX/LaTeX source (must submit TeX source)
- Scanned documents, regardless of format

## Figure formats
- PostScript (PS, EPS) — requires LaTeX
- JPEG, GIF, PNG, PDF — requires PDFLaTeX
- Submissions with omitted figures NOT accepted (even with external links)

## File name requirements
- Allowed: a-z A-Z 0-9 _ + - . , =
- Case-sensitive

## Submission requirements
- Only registered authors may submit
- **New users may require endorsement** (see `endorsement.md`)
- All submissions undergo moderation
- Authors must grant irrevocable distribution license
- Mandatory agreement to Terms of Service, code of conduct, moderation policy, privacy policy
- Self-submission expected; third-party only under specific conditions
- Submission before 14:00 ET typically announced at 20:00 ET

## Required metadata
- Title and abstract per separate metadata instructions
- Used for abstract pages, announcements, RSS, search
- Category (e.g., math.NT, math.MP, hep-th) required

## Back-catalog submission rate limit
> Authors are asked not to exceed three papers per day.
