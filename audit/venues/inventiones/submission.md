## General
- Springer journal.
- One of the top-tier mathematics journals (alongside Annals of Mathematics, JAMS).
- Publishes research articles in pure mathematics.

## Submission
- Manuscripts submitted via Springer's Editorial Manager.
- LaTeX strongly preferred; Springer's template recommended.
- English-language submissions.

## Format
- Research articles.
- Detailed proofs required.
- No strict page limit (typical papers 20-80 pages).

## Review process
- Single-blind peer review.
- Handled by Springer's editorial system with named editorial board.
- Review times can be lengthy (6-18 months typical for top-tier math journals).
