# Physical Review D / Letters — Submission Guidelines

**Source:** https://journals.aps.org/prd/authors (403 on 2026-04-14) + https://journals.aps.org/prl/authors + https://journals.aps.org/authors/length-guide + https://prl.aps.org/info/infoL.html
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## Physical Review D (PRD)
- APS journal.
- Topics: particles, fields, gravitation, cosmology.
- LaTeX REVTeX 4.2 strongly preferred.
- Standard research article length.
- Letters-within-PRD have 5-page length limit.

## Physical Review Letters (PRL)
- Flagship APS letters journal.
- **Length limit: 3750 words** (body, not counting abstract/authors/refs), approximately 4 pages.
- **End Matter:** up to 2 pages of appendices, does not count toward length.
- Comments: 750 words max.
- At submission, authors provide a **100-word justification** explaining why the paper meets PRL's criteria.

## Submission
- LaTeX source (preferred) or Word; PDF required for peer review.
- Submit via APS submission system.

## Clay Qualifying Outlet status
**NO (strictly).** Physics journals, not mathematics. See JHEP note.
