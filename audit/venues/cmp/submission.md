# Communications in Mathematical Physics (CMP) — Submission Guidelines

**Source:** https://link.springer.com/journal/220/submission-guidelines
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## General
- Springer journal.
- Leading international journal at the interface of mathematics and physics.
- Established 1965.
- Typical topics: QFT, statistical mechanics, operator algebras, mathematical aspects of string theory, rigorous mathematical physics.

## Manuscript format
- LaTeX strongly preferred.
- Springer Nature's LaTeX template recommended.
- Submit original source (style files + figures) AND PDF.

## Structure
- Decimal headings, no more than three levels.
- Abbreviations defined at first mention.
- Footnotes acceptable.

## General requirements (verbatim)
> Submission of a manuscript implies that the work described has not been published before; that it is not under consideration for publication anywhere else; that its publication has been approved by all co-authors, if any.

## Clay Qualifying Outlet status
**YES.** Named editorial board, Springer peer review, MathSciNet-indexed, worldwide repute.
