# Journal of High Energy Physics (JHEP) — Submission Guidelines

**Source:** https://jhep.sissa.it/jhep/help/helpLoader.jsp?pgType=author + https://link.springer.com/journal/13130/submission-guidelines
**LaTeX author manual:** https://jhep.sissa.it/jhep/help/JHEP/TeXclass/DOCS/JHEP-author-manual.pdf
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## General
- Fully open-access, single-blind peer-reviewed.
- **Fully sponsored by SCOAP3** (no APC for authors).
- Owned by SISSA (Trieste, Italy); published by Springer.
- Covers all of theoretical and experimental high-energy physics.

## Submission process
- Via JHEP Editorial Office at SISSA Medialab.
- Registration free.
- LaTeX with the JHEP class file (template available on Overleaf).

## Timeline
- Average 12 weeks submission-to-publication.

## Format requirements
- Authors specified via `\author[]{...}` and `\affiliation[]{...}` macros.
- Follow JHEP LaTeX author manual.

## Clay Qualifying Outlet status
**NO (strictly).** JHEP is a physics journal, not a mathematics publication. Clay §6e requires "refereed **mathematics** publication of worldwide repute" and MathSciNet inclusion. JHEP is indexed in Inspire HEP / Web of Science / MathSciNet (some articles).

**For physics-heavy programme papers** (Papers 2-7, 11-12, framework papers): JHEP is the primary target.
**For Clay submissions:** use a mathematics journal (Annals, Inventiones, JAMS, CMP).
