# Breakthrough Prize in Fundamental Physics — Rules

**Source:** https://breakthroughprize.org/Rules/1
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## Prize amount
$3 million main prize. $100,000 New Horizons in Physics. Prize can be SHARED among any number of scientists.

## Eligibility
- For outstanding contributions to fundamental physics (theoretical or experimental).
- **No age restrictions.** New Horizons has "no more than 12 years past PhD" constraint (with exceptions for long-term experimental projects).
- Recipients may win multiple times.

## Nominations
- **Self-nominations NOT allowed.**
- Anyone except the candidate may nominate via the online form during the open nomination window.
- A valid nomination requires:
  - Biographical info on nominee and nominator
  - At least one third-party letter of recommendation
  - Up to 10 citations from a specified academic database

## Special prize
- An exceptional Special Prize may be awarded at any time, bypassing the nomination process.

## Selection
- Past recipients serve on the Selection Committee.
