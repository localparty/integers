## Nomination rules
- **The right to nominate is open to anyone.**
- **Self-nominations NOT permitted.**
- Nominations are CONFIDENTIAL; the nominee must not be informed.
- Nominations submitted via the online form during the nomination window (typically closing mid-September for following year's prize).

## Eligibility
- Nominee must be ALIVE.
- Minimum requirement: candidate must have authored at least one scientific mathematical article in a peer-reviewed research journal.
- The prize may be awarded to a single person or shared for closely related fundamental contributions.

## Abel Committee
- Five outstanding research scientists in mathematics.
- Both Norwegian and non-Norwegian citizens may serve.
- Two-year term, reappointable once.
- Appointments based on recommendation by the Academy's mathematical group, selected from suggestions by IMU and EMS.

## Criteria
The Abel committee assesses:
- Whether the mathematical work is compliant with the Abel Prize statutes
- Academic quality
- Academic width

## Implications for QG5D programme
- The Abel Prize is lifetime-achievement in flavor but awarded for specific contributions.
- Any of the major programme proofs (RH, YM, BSD, PvNP) could anchor an Abel nomination.
- Requires a nominator — researcher network needed.
