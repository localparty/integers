# Fields Medal — Rules

**Source:** https://www.mathunion.org/imu-awards/fields-medal + https://www.mathunion.org/fileadmin/IMU/Prizes/Fields/Statutes%20Fields%20Medal.pdf
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## The age constraint (the critical rule)
> **A candidate's 40th birthday must not occur before January 1st of the year of the Congress at which the Fields Medals are awarded.**

In practice: recipients must be UNDER 40 on 1 January of the Congress year. The Congress is held every four years (next 2026, then 2030).

## Nomination
- Nominations to the committee's chair.
- Nominations are CONFIDENTIAL; "must not be disclosed to the candidate."
- The Fields Medal Committee is chosen by the Executive Committee of the IMU.
- Chair is publicly named; other members confidential until prize awarded.

## Prize details
- 2-4 medals awarded per Congress.
- Individual award, not shared.
- The prize is person-oriented, not paper-oriented — "for outstanding mathematical achievement for existing work and for the promise of future achievement."
