# Shaw Prize in Mathematical Sciences — Rules

**Source:** https://www.shawprize.org/nomination/
**Retrieved:** 2026-04-14

## Nomination rules
- **Nomination is BY INVITATION ONLY.**
- Self-nominations NOT permitted.
- Letters of reference, nominations by uninvited persons, and nominations by immediate family members are NOT considered.
- Each nominator may make ONLY ONE nomination citing one contribution.
- All nominations are strictly confidential.

## Timeline
- Chair of Selection Committee sends invitations in EARLY SEPTEMBER.
- Deadline: 30 NOVEMBER each year.
- Nominations deliberated for the following year's prize.

## Eligibility
- Nominators include:
  - Laureates of major prizes
  - Scientists and senior personnel of world's key research institutions / universities
  - Members of National Academies of Sciences and professional organizations
- Nomination of a DECEASED person NOT considered.
- Members of the Selection Committee, Board of Adjudicators, and Shaw Prize Council are INELIGIBLE.

## Prize amount
US$1.2 million (shared if multiple laureates).
