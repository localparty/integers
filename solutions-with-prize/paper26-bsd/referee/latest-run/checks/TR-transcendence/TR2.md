# Check TR2: log N_1 / log N_2 transcendental

**Group:** TR
**Source:** Paper 26 Proposition 8.4
**Pass criterion:** Transcendence for distinct Gaussian prime norms.

**Verdict:** PASS
**Rigor label:** [THEOREM]

**Justification:** For distinct positive integer prime norms
N_1 ≠ N_2, unique factorization gives irrationality, and Baker
upgrades to transcendence. Standard.

**Note:** Paper's Table 8.1 has numerical errors in 4 of 5
entries (audit C3), but this does not affect the transcendence
conclusion — the ratios are still transcendental; the specific
30-digit values reported in the table are just wrong.
