# Check TR6: Two bridge primes with distinct norms suffice

**Group:** TR
**Source:** Paper 26 §8.3, §8.5
**Pass criterion:** The argument needs only two primes.

**Verdict:** PASS
**Rigor label:** [LEMMA]

**Justification:** Standard. The first-order argument uses the
ratio of cocycle shifts at two primes; the ratio involves
log N_1 / log N_2. Two primes with distinct norms suffice for
the transcendence kill. More primes are not needed.
