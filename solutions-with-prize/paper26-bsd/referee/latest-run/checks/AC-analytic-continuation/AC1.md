# Check AC1: Analytic continuation of L(E, s)

**Group:** AC (Prerequisite: analytic continuation)
**Source:** Standard modularity (BCDT 2001); Hecke theta series for CM
**Pass criterion:** L(E, s) continues to all of ℂ.

**Verdict:** PASS
**Rigor label:** [THEOREM]

**Justification:** For CM curves, the Hecke theta series gives
a weight-2 cuspform whose L-function equals L(E, s). This
pre-dates Wiles and provides analytic continuation without
appealing to the full modularity theorem. For non-CM curves
BCDT 2001 applies, but the paper's scope is CM only.
