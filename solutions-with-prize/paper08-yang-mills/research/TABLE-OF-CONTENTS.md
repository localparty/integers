# The Yang-Mills Mass Gap on the Lattice: A Proof via Kaluza-Klein Topology

## Table of Contents — Final Reading Order

This paper proves that SU(N) lattice Yang-Mills theory confines and has
a mass gap at all couplings, using the Weitzenböck spectral gap on
CP^{N-1} to control a cluster expansion in the topologically trivial
sector.
