## New kill: K-8 "Transseries-reads-$C_N$ trap"

**Pattern category:** Structural-conflation (Borel-plane ambiguity magnitude vs Wilson coefficient)

**Statement:** An argument claiming the non-perturbative transseries structure of 4D SU(N) YM directly produces $C_N = 2(N^2-1)/\pi^6$ from renormalon cancellation conflates two structurally distinct objects:
- IR renormalon at Borel-plane $u=2$ → ambiguity magnitude ($\sim\Lambda^4/Q^4$, dimension-4 gluon condensate via Parisi-SVZ)

**Re-entry gate:** requires an independent derivation of $C_N$ from non-perturbative transseries structure; no candidate mechanism exists. Stokes constants for pure 4D SU(N) YM renormalons are currently unknown.
