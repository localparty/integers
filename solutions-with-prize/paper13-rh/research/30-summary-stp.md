## Result

The Gantmacher-Krein theorem gives: STP matrices have simple
eigenvalues with strict interlacing. Via DPTZ, this would give
the Arithmetic Theorem directly.

## Why it's a non-lead

B is NOT STP. The prime sum has oscillatory cos(n log p) entries
that break total positivity. The perturbation (~3.3) dwarfs the
spectral gaps (~10⁻¹·⁷ᴺ) for N ≥ 5. No perturbative rescue.

A continuous kernel total positivity route is unexplored but
speculative.
