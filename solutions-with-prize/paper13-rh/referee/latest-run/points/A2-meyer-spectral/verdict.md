# A2 Verdict: Meyer Spectral Inclusion

**Meyer's theorem:** Correctly cited. Distributional spectral inclusion is genuine.

**The upgrade to genuine eigenvalues:** NOT PROVED. The paper asserts "approximate spectrum preservation" but does not prove that distributional eigenstates yield approximate eigenvectors in H_1.

**Meyer-Nelson compatibility:** Conflates two different operators (Meyer's scaling operator vs. Axiom 1's T_BC). The identification is assumed, not proved.

**Comparison to Connes:** The fact that Connes has not achieved this upgrade in 25+ years is strong evidence of a genuine difficulty that the paper does not address.

**Overall: FAIL.** This is the primary gap in the proof. The upgrade from distributional to genuine spectrum is the fundamental open problem in the BC approach to RH, and this paper assumes rather than proves it (via Axiom 1).
