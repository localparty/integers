## 3. The two gaps

### Gap 1: Spectral convergence
**Statement:** μ_k(λ_N, N) → γ_k as N → ∞
**Evidence:** CCM numerics: 10⁻⁵⁵ precision at N ≈ 100
**Obstruction:** ITPFI gives weak-* convergence (state level),
not norm-resolvent convergence (operator level). Perturbation
norm α = 1/2 (borderline, Kato fails). CF convergence mechanism
not yet made rigorous.
**What would close it:** Norm-resolvent convergence of D(λ_N,N),
or a compactness/trapping argument.

### Gap 2: Spectral realisation
**Statement:** eigenvalues of D_∞ = zeros of ζ (Hilbert-Pólya)
**Evidence:** Connes programme (25 years), Meyer inclusion, CCM
numerics, ITPFI structural identification
**Obstruction:** The H₁ vs H_R wall (for BC approaches);
for CCM it's the identification of D_∞ with ζ
**What would close it:** The explicit formula as an operator
trace identity (Connes' strategy), or a new construction
