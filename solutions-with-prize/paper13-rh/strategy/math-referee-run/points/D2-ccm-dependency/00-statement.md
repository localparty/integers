# D2 — CCM Dependency and Honest Accounting [MEDIUM]

## The dependence

Paper 13's Layer 1 is **entirely CCM** (arXiv:2511.22755). The
load-bearing results are:

- CCM Theorem 4.2 (self-adjointness via Carathéodory-Fejér)
- CCM Theorem 5.10 (parts (i), (ii), (iii))
- CCM Lemma 7.2 (Meixner-Schäfke bound on prolate eigenfunctions)
- CCM Lemma 7.3 (k̂_λ → Ξ uniformly on substrips)
- CCM Proposition 3.3 (lower boundedness of QW_λ)

If any of these is wrong, the chain collapses.

## Paper 13's honest accounting

The paper rates itself 8/10, with the 2-point gap attributed to
CCM preprint status. Section 13.6:

> "Overall 8/10. Layer 1 (CCM operators) 8/10: Preprint, not yet
> refereed."

This is honest in the sense that it acknowledges the dependence.
Whether 8/10 is the correct number is the subject of D1 (my
overall assessment is 6-7/10 as written).
