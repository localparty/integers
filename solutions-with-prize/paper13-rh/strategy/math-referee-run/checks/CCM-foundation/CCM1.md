# CCM1 — D_N correctly defined on E_N^+

**Claim:** Paper 13's Layer 1 construction of D_N on the even
sector E_N^+ is consistent with the CCM framework.

**Pass criterion:** CCM construction verified; even-sector
restriction is consistent.

**Finding:** The even sector E_N^+ is a **modification** of CCM,
not a standard CCM object. CCM works on the full Sonin space E_N.
Paper 13 restricts to E_N^+ in Section 12 because the
even-simple hypothesis of CCM Theorem 5.10 does not hold on the
full sector (the minimum eigenvector alternates parity in N).

Paper 13 asserts (Sec 12.4): "CCM's construction preserves the
γ-symmetry, so the restriction is clean … Theorem 5.10 applies
to the restricted operators without modification." This is
asserted but not proved. The compatibility requires:
- parity commutation with QW_λ^N,
- structure of Dirichlet kernel η on E_N^+,
- even-sector analogue of CCM identification theorem.
