the integers.

Each step is a theorem. Each step is a derivation. There is no

```

```

Each arrow is rigorous (or to be made rigorous). Without the

What the framework adds is the SPECIFIC connection to arithmetic
via Identity 12. Other approaches might find similar connections,

### Not "the universe is deterministic"

Quantum mechanics remains true in the framework. Indeterminacy
arises from the e-coordinate sampling at measurement (Paper 1).
The framework doesn't eliminate quantum probabilities; it derives
them from the geometry.

The "determinism" is at the level of the LAWS (which are forced
by arithmetic), not at the level of individual events (which
remain quantum).
