# Paper 5 — Figures List

Figures to be created for Paper 5. These are descriptions and
specifications; actual figure files have not yet been produced.

---

## Figure 1: The Holonomy Correspondence Diagram

**Description:** Three-panel diagram showing the compact spaces S1, S2, CP2
with their respective gauge groups, holonomy types, and physical consequences.
Left panel: S1 with U(1) Aharonov-Bohm phase arrow. Middle: S2 with SU(2)
Higgs VEV. Right: CP2 with SU(3) flux tube cross-section.

**Section:** Section 2 (Geometric Origin of Color Confinement)

**Type:** Schematic / conceptual diagram

## Figure 2: The CP2 Flux Tube

**Description:** Cross-section of the confining flux tube as a projection
of the CP1 non-contractible 2-cycle from CP2 down to 4D. Shows the
chromo-electric field lines confined to a tube of radius ~1/Lambda_QCD.
The CP2 2-cycle wraps around the tube as a fiber.

**Section:** Section 3 (String Tension and Regge Trajectories)

**Type:** Geometric illustration

## Figure 3: Quark Mass Prediction vs Experiment

**Description:** Log-scale bar chart with six bars for u, d, s, c, b, t
quarks. Each bar shows the geometric prediction (with +/-20% uncertainty
band from the warp factor precision) vs the PDG measured value.
Shows the exponential hierarchy from geometry.

**Section:** Section 5 (Quark Mass Hierarchy)

**Type:** Data comparison chart

## Figure 4: String Tension from Regge Trajectories

**Description:** Regge plot (J vs M^2) for the leading meson and baryon
trajectories. Shows the geometric prediction alpha' = 1/(2*pi*sigma_geom)
as a straight line, compared to the experimental Regge slope from PDG.

**Section:** Section 3 (String Tension and Regge Trajectories)

**Type:** Data comparison

## Figure 5: The Glueball Spectrum Tower

**Description:** Mass spectrum of predicted CP2 glueballs (k=1,2,3,...)
compared to lattice QCD glueball predictions and experimental candidates
f_0(1500), f_0(1710), etc. Shows the tower spacing from CP2 harmonics
with ratio m_{k+1}/m_k = sqrt((k+1)(k+3)/(k(k+2))).

**Section:** Section 7 (Predictions and Experimental Tests)

**Type:** Spectrum comparison
