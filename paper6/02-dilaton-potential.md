# 2. The Dilaton Potential

## 2.1 The Two Terms

The dilaton potential is the sum of two contributions, both
derived in Paper 1:

**The Casimir term** (Paper 1, В§6.6):

    V_Casimir(ПҶ) = VвӮҖ/ПҶвҒҙ

This is the vacuum energy of bulk fields (graviton + 3 bulk
right-handed neutrinos) on the e-circle of radius `R = RвӮҖПҶ`.
It scales as `1/RвҒҙ вҲқ 1/ПҶвҒҙ` вҖ” a steep, repulsive potential that
dominates at small `ПҶ` (small e-circle radius).

**The Goldberger-Wise term** (Paper 1, В§6.6; Goldberger & Wise 1999):

    V_GW(ПҶ) = A ПҶвҒҙ (ln ПҶ)ВІ

This is the stabilization potential from a bulk scalar with boundary
conditions on the ZвӮӮ orbifold fixed points. It grows at large `ПҶ`
(large radius), providing the restoring force that prevents the
e-circle from expanding indefinitely.

**The full potential:**

    V(ПҶ) = VвӮҖ/ПҶвҒҙ + A ПҶвҒҙ (ln ПҶ)ВІ

## 2.2 The Minimum

The minimum at `ПҶ = ПҶ_min = 1` (by normalization convention)
satisfies `V'(ПҶ_min) = 0`:

    вҲ’4VвӮҖ/ПҶ_minвҒө + 4AПҶ_minВі(ln ПҶ_min)ВІ + 2AПҶ_minВі ln ПҶ_min = 0

At `ПҶ_min = 1`: `ln(1) = 0`, so the equation reduces to
`VвӮҖ = 0` вҖ” which means we need `ПҶ_min` slightly away from 1.
The exact location depends on the ratio `VвӮҖ/A`. The minimum is
at `ПҶ_min` where the Casimir repulsion balances the GW attraction.

The mass at the minimum:

    m_ПҶВІ = V''(ПҶ_min) ~ VвӮҖ/ПҶ_minвҒ¶ ~ в„Ҹc/RвӮҖвҒҙ Г— (RвӮҖ/l_Pl)ВІ

For `RвӮҖ вүҲ 10 Ојm`: `m_ПҶ ~ 10вҖ“20 meV` (Paper 1, Appendix I).

## 2.3 The Three Regimes вҖ” Mapped onto Cosmology

**Regime I: The Casimir plateau** (`ПҶ вүӘ ПҶ_min`)

For `ПҶ вҶ’ 0`, the potential approaches `V вҶ’ VвӮҖ/ПҶвҒҙ вҶ’ вҲһ`. But for
moderate `ПҶ вүӘ ПҶ_min`, the potential is nearly flat: `V вүҲ VвӮҖ Г—`
(slowly varying function). This flat plateau supports slow-roll
inflation. The e-circle is LARGE during inflation вҖ” the fifth
dimension is macroscopic.

**Regime II: The oscillation basin** (`ПҶ вүҲ ПҶ_min`)

After inflation, the dilaton rolls into the potential minimum and
oscillates. These oscillations are the reheating phase вҖ” the
dilaton's kinetic energy is converted to particle production
through its gravitational coupling to the Standard Model. The
e-circle settles to its equilibrium size `RвӮҖ вүҲ 10 Ојm`.

**Regime III: The thawing slope** (`ПҶ > ПҶ_min`)

At late times (the current epoch), the dilaton has rolled slightly
past the minimum and is slowly climbing the GW potential on the
other side. This slow roll produces the dark energy equation of
state `wвӮҖ = вҲ’0.85`, `w_a = вҲ’0.23` (Paper 2, Appendix F). The
e-circle is slowly growing вҖ” the fifth dimension is expanding.
