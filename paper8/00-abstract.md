## Abstract

Papers 1-7 establish the 11D e-dimension framework and derive the

accessible or near-future experimental precision. Eight precision

KK zero mode, giving `α = −1/6`. This differs from loop quantum
gravity (`α = −1/2`) and string theory (`α = −3/2`) — providing a
potentially decisive test for any future measurement.

**2. KK graviton corrections to the CMB power spectrum:**

    δC_ℓ^{TT}/C_ℓ^{TT} ~ (H_inf/M_KK)² × (something)

correction is at the `10⁻²⁰` level — unmeasurable. For the e-circle

The CMB is clean — no KK contamination at current or foreseeable
