**The question:**
The formula is derived from the Euler factor ratio Z_p(1+2delta)/Z_p(1). Is this derivation from Bost-Connes first principles, or does it use additional assumptions?

2. Evaluate the KMS state on the Hecke operator.

4. Compute the effective temperature shift for off-line zeros.
5. Form the ratio.
6. Simplify the numerator.

Each step is elementary algebra. The derivation is correct and self-contained.
